  • About

Parking Spot App is an effective solution to identify the availability of a particular parking slot in advance without going to that place. This would also save time and money of its users as they don’t need to go around the same place to find out an available parking slot. When the availability of a particular parking slot is identified, you can leisurely do shopping without worrying about parking your vehicle. Since this application is designed to check the availability of a particular secure parking space, you don’t need worry for your vehicle’s safety.
